## 1. Introduction

BlockCraft: Cube Master ("we", "our", "the game") is a mobile puzzle game
published by **[Your Studio Name]** ("we", "us"). This Privacy Policy
describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you play the game.

## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 Information you provide

- **Display name and avatar** — only if you choose to sign in with Google Play
  Games. You may use a random nickname and the default avatar to play without
  providing personal data.
- **Support emails** — if you contact us via the in-game support link, we
  receive whatever you choose to send.

### 2.2 Information collected automatically

- **Device identifiers** — we use Android's `SSAID` (Settings.Secure.ANDROID_ID)
  as a stable, non-personal identifier to associate your save data and
  progress. We do **not** access the device IMEI or MAC address.
- **Gameplay telemetry** — anonymous events such as "level_started", "level
  ended with score N", "IAP_purchase with sku X". These contain no personal
  data.
- **Crash reports** — Crashlytics collects device model, OS version, and stack
  traces from crashes. You can opt out by disabling analytics in **Settings →
  Privacy → Share analytics**.
- **Advertising identifiers** — Google AdMob reads the Google Advertising ID
  to deliver ads. You can reset this ID or opt out of personalized ads in
  **Settings → Privacy → Personalized ads** or in your device's Google
  Settings.

### 2.3 Information we do NOT collect

- We do not collect your name, email, phone number, address, contacts, photos,
  microphone input, location, or calendar.
- We do not record gameplay video or audio.
- We do not track you across other apps or websites.

## 3. How we use information

We use the information above to:

- Save and restore your game progress on this device and (if signed in) across
  devices via cloud sync.
- Deliver in-app purchases and verify receipts.
- Show you ads via Google AdMob.
- Diagnose crashes and improve the game.
- Maintain leaderboards and award achievements.
- Provide customer support.

We **do not** sell your information, and we **do not** allow third parties to
sell your information.

## 5. Children

The game is rated for general audiences. We do **not** knowingly collect
personal data from children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR). The game is
configured to treat all players as child-directed for advertising purposes
(`TagForChildDirectedTreatment = True`), which disables interest-based
advertising. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us
and we will delete it.

## 6. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or California, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Delete** your data — see **Settings → Privacy → Delete save data**.
- **Object** to processing — see **Settings → Privacy → Share analytics**.
- **Port** your data — sign in to Google Play Games; your data syncs via
  Firestore to the account you sign in with.
- **Withdraw consent** at any time, including for personalized advertising.

To exercise these rights, contact **privacy@[your-domain]**. We respond
within 30 days.

## 7. Data retention

- **Save data** — kept on your device until you uninstall the game or tap
  **Delete save data** in Settings.
- **Cloud save** — kept in Firestore until you delete your Firebase account
  or until 24 months of inactivity, whichever comes first.
- **Analytics events** — kept 14 months in aggregate.
- **Crash reports** — kept 90 days.
- **Advertising data** — Google retains per its own policies
  (https://policies.google.com/technologies/retention).

## 8. International transfers

We use Google Cloud services, which transfer data to the United States. By
using the game you consent to this transfer. Google's EU/Swiss Privacy Shield
and Standard Contractual Clauses apply.

## 9. Security

Save data is encrypted with AES-GCM (256-bit). The encryption key is derived
from the Android device ID and a per-build salt; rotate the salt between
versions to invalidate tampered saves. All network calls go through HTTPS.

the most recent change. Material changes are announced in-game on the next
